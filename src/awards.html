The Awards

Celebrating excellence in Artificial Intelligence.

Overview

The Bharat AI Innovation Awards recognize the most groundbreaking AI solutions and the visionaries behind them.

Award Categories

Best AI Startup

For startups less than 3 years old with a disruptive AI product.

AI for Social Good

Solutions addressing critical social challenges in health, education, or environment.

Best Enterprise AI

Large-scale AI implementations driving business value.

Woman in AI

Recognizing outstanding female leadership in the AI sector.

Academic Research

Breakthrough research papers and academic contributions.

Best AI Hardware

Innovations in chips, sensors, and edge devices.

Judging Process

Nomination

Submit your entry online.

Screening

Initial review by expert panel.

Jury Round

Deep dive presentation to the grand jury.

Award Ceremony

Winners announced at the gala night.
